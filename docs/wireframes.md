# Phase 1 Wireframes

## Public landing

- Hero with dreamy gradients, floating planets, and two CTA paths: `Log in` and `Create your universe`
- Setup notice appears when Supabase env values are missing

## Auth

- Split layout with feature teaser on one side and frosted auth card on the other
- Signup includes nickname, emoji identity, avatar, and theme preview selection

## Universe shell

- Floating header with presence, pairing status, and notifications
- Mobile-first bottom navigation for `Space`, `Ideas`, `Chat`, `Play`, `Memories`, and `Settings`

## Space

- Mood-based hero recommendation card
- Pairing card or partner summary
- Dream board highlights
- Live vote panel
- Random picker wheel
- Cozy chat preview
- Memory timeline preview

## Ideas

- Filters, search, and playful category chips
- Create-idea panel with image URL, tags, and shortlist controls

## Play

- Voting, spin wheel, and mini-game deck in one joyful playground

## Chat

- Realtime conversation with presence, typing, reactions, and read receipts

## Memories

- Illustrated timeline that blends manual memories with completed ideas and milestones
